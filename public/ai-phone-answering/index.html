Common Questions

FAQ

What does this cost?

Setup is a one-time custom build (priced per project — most small businesses land between $2,500-$5,000 depending on complexity). Monthly Twilio costs are usually $30-$100 depending on call volume. No monthly fee from me after the build is done; you own the system. If you want me on retainer for tuning, that's optional.

How is this different from a virtual receptionist service?

A receptionist service charges per call, per minute, or per month — forever — and uses generic scripts. The AI agent I build is custom code that lives on your Twilio number. After the build, your only ongoing cost is Twilio itself (cents per minute). And it doesn't get tired, sick, or take lunch.

Will callers know it's AI?

Modern voice AI sounds nearly indistinguishable from a human for short interactions. We can configure it to disclose ("Hi, you've reached the AI assistant for ABC Plumbing") or not — your call. For most small businesses I recommend disclosing once at the start and then letting it do its job.

Does it work in Spanish?

Yes. The agent auto-detects the caller's language and responds in English or Spanish. Critical for the San Antonio market.

What if it screws up an important call?

Two safety nets. First: the agent transfers any caller who asks for a human or says anything urgent to your cell phone — instantly. Second: you get the full transcript and audio of every call, so you can review what happened. For the first 30 days I monitor closely and tune the system any time we hit an edge case.
