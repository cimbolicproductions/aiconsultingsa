The problem

Voicemail is not a sales process.

A missed call usually means the customer keeps moving down Google results. AI phone answering gives the caller an immediate response and gives you structured details instead of another vague voicemail.

The goal is not to pretend AI is human. The goal is to keep the lead warm, capture the job details, and route the right calls to you when a human needs to step in.
